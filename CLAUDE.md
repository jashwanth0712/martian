# Project Goal

Transform this 3D driving game into a Mars-themed experience.
The player should feel like they are driving on Mars.

Earth-specific elements (green grass, trees, flowers, Earth weather, urban furniture like benches/bricks)
should be replaced or removed in favour of Martian equivalents:
- Red/orange dust and rocky terrain
- Dust storms instead of rain/snow
- Craters and Martian rock formations
- No vegetation (no trees, flowers, bushes, grass)
- Reddish atmospheric haze and lighting
- No social media elements (statues, logos, links) — the Social area must remain disabled
